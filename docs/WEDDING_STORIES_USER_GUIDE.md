# Wedding Stories — Admin Guide

Open **Admin → Wedding Stories** in the sidebar.

## The golden rule (same as everywhere in the CMS)

Changes are saved as **drafts** and are not live until you **Publish**.

## Editing the hero

The top card edits the page banner: background image, subtitle, two title lines, and an overlay
darkness slider (1.00 = current look). **Save Draft** to keep your work, **Publish Hero** to go live.

## Managing stories

- **Add Story** — creates a blank story and scrolls to it.
- **Edit** (chevron) — expands the editor: names, date, location, theme, palette, two paragraphs
  (600 characters each), and four images.
- **Four images per story:** Main (large), then the three-up grid. Upload each independently; they go
  to Cloudinary automatically.
- **Publish** — puts that story on the live page. **Save Draft** keeps it hidden.
- **Duplicate** — copies everything as a new draft (fresh images are shared, not re-uploaded).
- **Delete** — moves the story to **Trash** at the bottom. **Restore** brings it back as a draft, or
  **Delete Forever** removes it permanently.
- **Drag** the ⣿ handle to reorder — the live page follows this order.

## Common questions

**"I edited a story but the website looks the same."** You saved a draft — press Publish.
**"I deleted a story and it's still showing."** Deletes are instant for archived, but a published
story you archived should disappear on publish/refresh; check it's in Trash.
**"My image didn't change."** Wait for the upload bar to finish, then Publish.
